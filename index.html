Research

I mainly work on cosmic reionization and galaxy formation. I work with optical/near-infrared data obtained with Subaru, JWST, etc...

Cosmic Reionization and Galaxy Formation

Cosmic reionization is the last phase change of our universe that ended around redshit 5. With the onset of the first light (i.e., stars, galaxies), ionizing photons produced by baryonic structures ionize the intergalactic neutral hydrogen gas. This cosmic scale astrophysical phenomenon thus imprints the formation of the first stars as well as the evolution of cosmic structures. My goal is to fully capture this cosmological event in the context of structure formation via observations.
